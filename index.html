What is English Finish?

Copy editing or proofreading?

While there is an overlap between the two, the terms are not synonymous. When you submit a manuscript for publication it is first copy edited, then proofread. In the process, the copy editor will overhaul your manuscript so that the proofreader can run the final check and eliminate any remaining errors before your work is sent to the printer. Find out more about the copy editing and proofreading services offered by English Finish to decide which service you need.
